 December 2018 The fourth quarter of 2018 saw substantial shifts in global asset prices sentiment and fundamentals global growth went from a 3.5% pace to tracking closer to 2.7% the market went from expecting nearly three Fed hikes in 2019 to zero and the S P 500 declined nearly 20% from late September to late December while U.S. Treasury yields fell to January 2018 levels.1 In light of this in this month s letter we review our outlook for key investment themes heading into 2019.
