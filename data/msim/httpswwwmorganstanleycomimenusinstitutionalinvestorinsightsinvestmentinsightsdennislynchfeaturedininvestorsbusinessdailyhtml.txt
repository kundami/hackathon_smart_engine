 Dennis Lynch Head of Counterpoint Global at Morgan Stanley Investment Management was recently highlighted in the Investor s Business Daily article These 2 Hot Sectors Help Morgan Stanley Stay Among Top Mutual Funds. The article highlights Dennis and the Morgan Stanley Institutional Fund Growth Portfolio which was the top performing fund in three categories U.S. Diversified Stock Growth Stock and Large Cap Funds for the 10 year period ending December 31 2018. When describing the team s award winning strategy Dennis Lynch head of Counterpoint Global told IBD We invest in companies that we believe have strong sustainable competitive advantages and large end game potential. We combine both fundamental analysis and broader disruptive change research to identify companies that we believe either benefit from disruption or that are relatively insulated from it.
